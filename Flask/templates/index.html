Restaurant Recommendation System

Build Recommendation System with ease!!

In today's era of information overload, individuals rely on various strategies to decide what to purchase, how to spend their free time, and where to go. Recommender systems help automate these decision-making processes, aiming to suggest affordable dining options. This project focuses on developing a content-based recommender system that, upon entering the name of a restaurant, analyzes reviews of other establishments and recommends those with similar feedback. The suggestions are then sorted based on their ratings, with the highest-rated options appearing first.
